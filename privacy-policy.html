Privacy Policy

Clear, practical privacy for every inquiry

This policy explains how MATTECHS Solutions collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information submitted through this website and our inquiry form.

At a glance

We use your information to respond to inquiries, discuss solutions, and support business conversations.

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Affiliate sharing happens only with your consent.
  • You can ask us to update or delete your details.
Effective date: June 10, 2026

1. Who we are

MATTECHS Solutions provides technology, engineering, manufacturing-aligned, AI-led, and strategic partnership solutions. In this policy, "MATTECHS", "we", "us", and "our" refer to MATTECHS Solutions.

2. Information we collect

When you submit an inquiry or request a callback, we may collect:

  • Your name, company name, email address, and phone number.
  • Your selected inquiry type and message details.
  • Any additional information you choose to include in your message.
  • Basic technical information processed by our website, hosting, form, email, or security providers.

3. How we use your information

We use submitted information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or callback request.
  • Understand your requirements and suggest suitable solutions.
  • Coordinate meetings, proposals, support, and partnership conversations.
  • Maintain internal business records and improve our website and services.
  • Comply with applicable legal, regulatory, or security obligations.

4. Consent and affiliate sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your details with relevant third-party affiliates, partners, vendors, or implementation specialists only when it is necessary for your inquiry and only with your consent.

If an affiliate or partner is better placed to support your requirement, we will use the consent provided through the inquiry form or a separate communication before sharing your details.

5. Service providers

We use service providers to operate this website and receive form submissions, including Web3Forms for inquiry submissions and email or hosting services for communication and storage. These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services to us.

6. Cookies and website data

This website may use basic technical tools, browser features, fonts, and hosting logs that help the site load, stay secure, and work correctly. We do not use this website to sell visitor data.

7. How long we keep information

We keep inquiry information for as long as needed to respond to you, maintain business records, support ongoing discussions, and meet legal or operational requirements. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. No online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to limit access to people and providers who need it for business purposes.

9. Your choices and rights

You may contact us to request that we:

  • Confirm whether we hold your personal information.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Delete your information where legally and operationally possible.
  • Withdraw consent for future affiliate sharing or non-essential contact.

10. Contact us

For privacy questions, correction requests, deletion requests, or consent withdrawal, contact us at sales@mattechsol.com.

Office address: C-403, Ruturang Apartments, S.No.41, Behind Paranjape School, Kothrud, Pune 411038.

11. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
